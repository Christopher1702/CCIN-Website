CCIN Capabilities

Formal Indicator Framework

Indicators are selected using defined criteria (relevance, measurability, forward impact). Each metric has documented assumptions and definitions.

Data Standardization Pipeline

Raw datasets are cleaned, normalized, and aligned across time horizons to ensure comparability between sectors.

Target Trajectory Modeling

For each indicator, the system calculates:

  • Required annual growth rate to hit policy targets
  • Actual growth rate
  • Deviation from required trajectory

Momentum Scoring Engine

Each sector receives a quantified performance score based on:

  • Current growth rate
  • Acceleration or deceleration trends
  • Target alignment
  • Statistical confidence

Forecasting and Risk Analysis

Time-series modeling projects forward trajectories under current momentum and identifies probability of shortfall.

Transparent Methodology Documentation

All equations, assumptions, and weighting logic are version-controlled and publicly documented. This creates credibility and defensibility.
